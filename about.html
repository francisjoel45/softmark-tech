Our Story

Founded in 2015, Softmark Tech began as a small team of passionate developers with a vision to create innovative IT solutions that make a real difference for businesses. What started as a modest startup has grown into a leading technology company serving clients across multiple industries.

Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a relentless focus on delivering exceptional value to our clients. We've stayed true to our core values while embracing new technologies and methodologies to stay at the forefront of the industry.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with innovative technology solutions that drive growth and efficiency.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted technology partner for businesses worldwide.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company's growth and development.

2015

Company Founded

Softmark Tech was established with a small team of 5 developers working from a shared office space.

2016

First Major Client

Secured our first enterprise client, marking our entry into the corporate IT solutions market.

2017

Expanded Services

Launched our web hosting division and expanded our development team to 15 members.

2018

New Headquarters

Moved to our current headquarters in Silicon Valley with a team of 30 professionals.

2020

Global Expansion

Opened our first international office in London and crossed 100 employees worldwide.

2023

Current Status

Now serving over 500 clients globally with a team of 150+ IT professionals and experts.
