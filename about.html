Our Journey

From startup to emerging industry leader - our growth story

2023

Company Founded

Softmark Tech was established by a group of industry veterans with a shared vision for better technology solutions. Our first office opened in San Francisco with just 5 team members.

2023

First Major Client

We secured our first enterprise client, validating our approach and marking our official entry into the corporate IT solutions market.

2024

Service Expansion

Launched our cloud solutions division and expanded our service offerings to meet growing client demands. Our team grew to 12 members.

2024

Industry Recognition
